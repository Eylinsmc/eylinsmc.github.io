About me

Hi, there! I am Eylin Canquiz. I started this passion for writing when I was very young.
Words have the power to connect with people's souls and minds. They allow you to describe moments forever,
to write the book of your life: memories narrated, photographs described, and if you add a little melody even music.

They also allow you to drain, to let go.
They are always with us, sometimes in sounds, in images but always summarized in words.

You know what they say: A word can say more than a thousand images, can't it?

Just kidding, but that's the magic. I hope you have smiled, at least.
This is a little part of me, of this art.
